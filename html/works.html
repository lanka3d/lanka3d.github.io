Works

3D-Model

Heii, these are my 3D-Models!

I worked with different 3D-Modelling Software, such as Cinema4D, Maya and Blender.

Using Cinema4D I modelled a scene from my favourite bookseries The Disc World by Terry Pratchett in Low Poly.

Using Maya I modelled an Original Character named Keket and a Drone in Dr. Suess style, both of which I conceptualized and designed first.

2D-Animation

Heii, these are my 2D-Animations!

I worked mostly with the Software Adobe After-Effects and Illustrator.

Using Illustrator I conceptualized and designed the different Animations and Assets. In After-Effects I learned and discovered the 12 principles of Animation in action.

The Video "One more Breath" is personally important to me because it visualizes the struggles patients with COPD, such as my mother face.

Games

Heii, these are my Games!

I worked with different Game Engines, such as Godot and Unity.

Soul Bundle is a project I conceptualized and implemented using Godot. It is a 2-player Coop-Game, both having different abilities and see different things while controlling the same character.

Blueprint to Freedom is a board game I conceptualized and designed. The game can be played with up to 5 people and everybody has their own plan to break out of prison they need different objects distributed on the gameplan

Poster
