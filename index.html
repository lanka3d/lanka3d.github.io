About me

Heii, I’m Leanne Klemm!

I’m 24 and living in a shared house in Trier, where I’m diving into all things creative as an Intermedia Design student.

With a background in IT I got a bit of a techy edge, but it’s the mix of creativity and logic that really lights me up. I’m fascinated by the space where digital meets design — how visuals, stories, and tech can come together to make something powerful.

When I’m not studying, you can find me travelling the world, reading about another one, sketching my latest ideas or letting my mind wander and dream.
